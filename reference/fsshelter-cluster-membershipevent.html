MembershipEvent Type

Namespace: FsShelter.Cluster
Assembly: FsShelter.dll
Base Type: obj
All Interfaces: IEquatable<MembershipEvent> , IStructuralEquatable , IComparable<MembershipEvent> , IComparable , IStructuralComparable
